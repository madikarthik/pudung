About Pudung Farms

Our Story and Philosophy

Nestled in the serene folds of Kalimpong's countryside, our farm is a living canvas of nature, tradition, and timeless wisdom. Here, every leaf, stone, and stream tells a story—of balance, of harmony, of the dignified simplicity that defines physical life close to the earth.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We practice organic farming and traditional methods that respect and preserve the land for future generations.

Community

We believe in the power of connection – between people, with animals, and with the land that sustains us all.

Authenticity

We preserve and share genuine rural experiences and traditional knowledge, keeping cultural heritage alive.
