Our Mission

Preserving Tradition, Nurturing Nature

At Pudung Farms, our mission is to preserve and share the authentic rural lifestyle of Kalimpong while promoting sustainable farming practices and cultural preservation. We believe in creating meaningful connections between people and the land, fostering an understanding of traditional wisdom, and supporting local communities.

Sustainability

We practice and promote organic farming methods that protect our environment and ensure long-term ecological balance.

Community

We work closely with local communities, supporting traditional livelihoods and preserving cultural heritage.

Education

We share knowledge about sustainable farming, traditional practices, and environmental conservation.

Authenticity

We offer genuine experiences that reflect the true essence of rural life in Kalimpong.
